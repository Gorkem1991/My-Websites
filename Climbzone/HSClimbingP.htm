Hen/Stag Climbing Parties

We can help you arrange a successful and entertaining Stag or Hen event.

We offer climbing packages for stag and hen groups of any size, age range and climbing ability, we add a competitive but fun element to your experience!

Our 3 hour Stag and Hen sessions will allow you to fully experience the excitement and exhilaration of climbing in the weather proof comfort of our state-of-the art indoor climbing centre.

Overcome your fears and master our 11m high climbing walls, before putting your trust in your friends as you let go and they lower you back to the ground. Our expert climbing instructors will keep you safe and reaching the top is guaranteed to give an unbeatable sense of accomplishment.

Experience the thrills of climbing without using a rope or harness within the Big Rock bouldering arena, our huge low level climbing area featuring a wall to wall cushion of thick foam padding. The short climbs in the arena offer intense challenges that require balance, agility and strength combined with problem solving abilities and the mental tenacity to succeed!

Male or female, confident or a little nervous, climbing at Big Rock provides an exhilarating and sociable activity for all abilities.

When
